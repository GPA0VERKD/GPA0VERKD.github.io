About Me

This page includes more info on who David Phan is, his background, hobbies, and other fun facts about him!

Background

I've lived in Seattle, WA for most of my life, and was raised with a Vietnamese-American upbringing. I've always been involved with the community through church, service groups like National Honor Society, and other miscellaneous activities. These helped me to hold a deep appreciation for family and community, and gave me the basis for my growing leadership skills in the future.

Hobbies

My hobbies mostly consist of playing music and videogames. I love co-op games since I always grew up playing with my older brother! I've also been playing music since I was young, but became interested in Jazz and more collaborative music during High School.
